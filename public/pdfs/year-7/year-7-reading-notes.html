üîç 2. Reading Skills for Year 7

Deeper Inference

Going beyond surface meaning to understand author's intent, tone, and purpose.

"The old lighthouse had stood abandoned for decades. Yet somehow, its light still flickered on stormy nights."
What does this suggest about the lighthouse?

Author's Purpose

  • Inform: Teach facts, explain
  • Persuade: Convince, influence
  • Entertain: Amuse, enjoy
  • Express: Share feelings, opinions

Analyzing Language

Identify figurative language and its effect:

  • Metaphor: Direct comparison ("Life is a journey")
  • Simile: Uses like/as ("Fast as lightning")
  • Personification: Human traits on non-human ("The wind whispered")
