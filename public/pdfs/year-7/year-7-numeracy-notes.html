üìå 1. Integers & Number Lines

Understanding Integers

Integers include negative numbers, zero, and positive numbers.

... -3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3 ...

Negative: Below zero (cold temperatures, debt)

Positive: Above zero (height, money you have)

Adding & Subtracting Integers

  • Same signs: Add, keep the sign (3 + 5 = 8)
  • Different signs: Subtract, keep the larger sign (7 + -3 = 4)
  • Subtracting: Change to adding the opposite (5 - -3 = 5 + 3 = 8)
Calculate: -5 + 8 = __________ | 3 - -4 = __________

üî¢ 3. Algebra

Writing Expressions

Translate words to algebra:

  • "5 more than x" ‚Üí x + 5
  • "3 times y" ‚Üí 3y
  • "subtract 4 from z" ‚Üí z - 4
  • "half of m" ‚Üí m/2
Write: "7 less than twice a number n"

Solving Equations

Use inverse operations to isolate the variable:

x + 5 = 12 ‚Üí x = 12 - 5 ‚Üí x = 7
2y = 18 ‚Üí y = 18 √∑ 2 ‚Üí y = 9
n/4 = 3 ‚Üí n = 3 √ó 4 ‚Üí n = 12

üìä 4. Ratio & Proportion

Understanding Ratio

Ratio compares quantities. Use : or "to"

Red to Blue = 3:2 | "3 boys to 2 girls"

Proportion

Proportion says two ratios are equal

If 2 apples cost $4, then 4 apples cost $8
1/2 = 2/4 (proportion)
