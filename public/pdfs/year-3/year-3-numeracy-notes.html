Multiplication Strategies

  • Doubles: 4 √ó 6 = 2 √ó 6 + 2 √ó 6 = 12 + 12 = 24
  • Fives: 5 √ó 7 = (5 √ó 5) + (5 √ó 2) = 25 + 10 = 35
  • Zeros: Any number √ó 0 = 0
  • Ones: Any number √ó 1 = itself
7 √ó 8 = 56 (remember this one!)

üìù 5. Word Problems

Addition Word Problems

Keywords: altogether, total, in all, sum, plus

Tom has 23 marbles. Sarah gives him 15 more. How many does Tom have now?
23 + 15 = 38 marbles
There are 45 apples in a basket. 28 more apples are added. How many apples are there now?

Subtraction Word Problems

Keywords: left, remaining, difference, minus, less

Sarah had 56 stickers. She gave 24 to her friend. How many are left?
56 - 24 = 32 stickers
A bookstore had 100 books. 37 were sold. How many books are left?

Multiplication Word Problems

Keywords: groups of, times, each, altogether, product

There are 4 boxes. Each box has 6 cookies. How many cookies altogether?
4 √ó 6 = 24 cookies
There are 7 rows of chairs. Each row has 8 chairs. How many chairs?
